Focus Areas in Parliament

Four recurring areas of engagement — women's empowerment, education, urban development and the environment — shaped by her record as an educationist and legislator.

Focus Area 01

Education & Academia

As a former Assistant Professor with a PhD in education, Dr. Kulkarni brings a classroom perspective to questions of curriculum, skilling and access to higher education — drawing on more than two decades in the teaching profession before her entry into full-time politics.

Engagement with educational institutions

Continued interaction with colleges and universities in Pune on matters of curriculum and skill development.

Ongoing

Skilling & digital literacy

Support for skilling and digital-literacy initiatives aligned with national education priorities.

Ongoing

Scholarship & access advocacy

Advocacy for improved access to higher education for students from the constituency.

Urban Development

Civic infrastructure follow-up

Continued attention to roads, water supply and civic infrastructure concerns raised by Kothrud residents.

Ongoing

Public transport & connectivity

Engagement on improving public transport connectivity in and around Pune.
